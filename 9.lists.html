Mythical creatures

dragon
a mythical monster resembling a giant reptile, sometimes shown as having wings. In European tradition the dragon is typically fire-breathing and tends to symbolize chaos or evil, whereas in East Asia it is usually a beneficent symbol of fertility, associated with water and the heavens.
phoenix
The phoenix is a mythical bird that symbolizes immortality, rebirth, and life after death. It is part of Greek mythology, but has similar depictions in Egyptian and Persian mythology
vampire
A vampire is a mythical creature that is said to feed on the vital essence of the living. In European folklore, vampires are often depicted as undead humanoids with fangs that come back to life at night to suck the blood of sleeping people.
werewolf
a werewolf is a man who turns into a dangerous animal at night
